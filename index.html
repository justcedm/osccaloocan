OSC ORIGIN
Okinawa Shorin ryu Clan (OSC) was founded on
May 6, 1975 at the Holy Rosary Major Seminary, Concepcion Heights, Naga City, Camarines Sur

Supreme Master Benedicto “Benny” P. Elopre, born on May 6, 1955, in Calabanga, Camarines Sur, Philippines, began his martial arts journey inspired by his boxing hero 'Flash' Elorde. His initial karate training was under Master Juanito Piaz during his elementary school years, and he continued to train diligently through high school, ultimately embracing the Okinawan Shorin-Ryu style. Over the years, he earned several black belt ranks and founded the Okinawa Shorin-Ryu Clan to share his knowledge with both the clergy and the wider community. Known for his visionary approach, Master Elopre combined karate's principles with his spiritual beliefs, focusing on holistic development. Now an 8th Dan Okinawan Red & White Belt, he has established many karate chapters across the Philippines and internationally, leaving a lasting impact on countless students.
